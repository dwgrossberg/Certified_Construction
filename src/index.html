Welcome to Our Company's Database Management System

This is the main portal to access and manage different aspects of our company's operations. Below, you'll find links to various pages that provide specific functionalities.

Pages and Functionalities

  • Departments Page

    View and manage department details. Add, edit, or remove departments as needed.

  • Certifications Page

    Manage certifications available to employees. Add new certifications, update existing ones, and handle certification details.

  • Employees Page

    Access and update employee records, add new employees, or edit information related to existing employees.

  • Training Sessions Page

    Schedule, update, and manage training sessions. Link sessions to certifications and manage dates and locations.

  • Employee Certifications Page

    Assign, update, and manage certifications for each employee. Keep track of certification expiry and renewals.

  • Employee Training Page

    Register employees for training sessions, manage session attendance, and view training schedules.
